Safalya can be achieved by perseverance. We, as Safalya, believe to reach the top of the staircase of success by following the steps of dedication, commitment and improvement. We are passionate about delivering our best innovative software solutions

A Brief History

Safalya has become a collaboration of brilliant-innovative minds along with a fun loving-friendly team,who work together towards company goals to fulfill clients satisfaction and expectations for their product. Our aim is to provide the best mobile and web applications with variety of domains. We always love to develop challenging and innovative applications that can bring the change into the world!

We Have Come A Long Way

  • Our Start
    Safalya was founded by Piyush Jadhav: A software development firm with the goal to provide customers better web & mobile solutions.
  • The Early Days
    We Started our journey in an apartment flat with the count of two interns, and few E-commerce and SAP projects in our hand
  • Working Flow
    We believe in product delivery by following tasks management in the appropriate way. We follow the Agile framework to achieve this. By regular standup meetings, task prioritisation, bugs tracking and CI/CD, we have been able to deliver our products according to timeline.
  • The Expansion
    We grew up with our sales and connected with more clients. We expanded the safalya family with more members designated as designers,developers and testers and hence expanded the office to a workspace.
  • At Present
    Today, We are having clients abroad. We have a handshake for more new projects all in different domains and growing! Having a team of 16 members and quickly growing!
  • Future plans
    We are planning to grow more in IOT, machine learning and AR development. We are also working on implementing our own products with our innovative ideas. We dream to expand and grow safalya among the best solution providers in the world!
